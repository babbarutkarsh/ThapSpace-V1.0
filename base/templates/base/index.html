So what is ThapSpace?

‘ThapSpace’ aims at making engineering students of Thapar University effectively utilize their 4 years in the college and to make them outshine from among the crowd of 1.5 million engineers graduating every year. The objective of our project is to develop a web app specifically for the students of TIET. As mentioned above the issues which are currently being faced by the students, so our web app shall include features like providing information at a common place regarding all the companies visiting the campus for placements or internships plus off campus notifications. It will also include a chatbot to allow juniors to interact with their seniors/alumni and discuss about projects, strategies to prepare and much more. Our web app shall display information of the placed or interned alumni in big companies. An additional feature of this web app includes a section which shall be dedicated to the Capstone Projects.

What made us do it?

We did a survey in Thapar University and asked students about the major issues faced by them in collge.

The results we got were that students are not having a proper guidance for placements and shockingly most of them were in their third year.

What we offer through ThapSpace?

  1. A platform to get in touch with your seniors
  2. Most Effective Ways to utilise your free time in University
  3. Tips on Starting with coding
  4. Basic and Advanced Interesting Python coding Projects
  5. Must Read books while in university to get better understanding of world after 4 years.
  6. Guidance about getting your dream Job
  7. Exclusive Interviews of students placed in companies visiting Your Campus.
